VERDICT ON NEWSAPI
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Useful but not essential given what we already have.

PROS:
  - description field is better than Google News title fragments
  - Massive index (27k+ results for iran hormuz)
  - Direct URL to articles (not Google redirect links)
  - Globalsecurity.org, Japan Today, Times of India not in other feeds

CONS:
  - 12-24 hour delay on free tier — not real-time
  - 100 req/day limit — 20 poll cycles maximum at 5 queries each
  - Mixed source quality — needs allowlist filtering
  - Content is truncated (no full body)
  - Reuters/Bloomberg/FT/AP are indexed but not reliably surfaced in
    top 10 results — dominated by smaller outlets on free tier

RECOMMENDATION: Add to collector with source quality filter and low
polling frequency (1-2x/day). Use primarily for daily sweep of
longer-tail sources not captured by RSS feeds, not as real-time signal.

REGISTER FOR: Mediastack (500/mo free) and TheNewsAPI as backups —
both may have better real-time performance than NewsAPI free tier.
